# Compliance Controls

## Positioning
BioPOS AuthBridge must be treated as a regulated financial authorization feature, not as a convenience-only biometric add-on.

## Core controls
- Explicit customer consent
- Risk-based onboarding
- Customer due diligence linkage
- Transaction monitoring
- Suspicious activity escalation
- Secure audit trail
- Data subject rights handling
- Biometric revocation process
- Processor and vendor due diligence
- Legal review for liability allocation

## Data handling rules
- Minimize sensitive data storage
- Do not store raw biometric images in ordinary operational systems
- Encrypt sensitive records at rest using AES-256-class controls
- Require TLS 1.2+ for all service and partner communications
